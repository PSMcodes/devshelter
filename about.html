Why Choose DevShelter ?

Comfort and Peace

At DEV SHELTERS, we prioritize your comfort and peace above all else. Our serviced apartments provide a serene environment, ensuring you have a restful stay after a long day of work or exploration. Each apartment is thoughtfully designed with modern amenities and cozy furnishings to make you feel right at home. Whether you are a corporate executive needing a quiet place to focus or a tourist looking for a relaxing retreat, our accommodations cater to your every need, offering a tranquil haven amidst the bustling city of Mumbai.

Cost-Effective Luxury

Enjoy all the luxuries of a high-end hotel without the exorbitant costs at DEV SHELTERS. Our serviced apartments offer a cost-effective alternative to traditional hotel stays, providing exceptional value for money. You'll have access to spacious living areas, fully equipped kitchens, and premium facilities, all at a fraction of the cost of a hotel. This makes our apartments ideal for both short-term stays and extended visits, ensuring you get the best of both worlds – luxury and affordability.

Prime Locations and Convenience

Strategically located in the prime areas of Malad, Goregaon, and Jogeshwari, our serviced apartments offer unparalleled convenience. These locations are surrounded by business hubs, shopping malls, and a variety of restaurants, ensuring you are never far from the action. Moreover, the excellent transport links make commuting to major spots in Mumbai a breeze. Whether you are here for business or leisure, our apartments provide easy access to everything you need, making your stay in Mumbai not only comfortable but also incredibly convenient.
